Cyber Security Awareness Articles

1. The Importance of Strong Passwords

Using strong, unique passwords is one of the easiest ways to protect your accounts from hackers. A strong password should include a combination of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Avoid using personal information like your name or birthdate.

2. Recognizing Phishing Attacks

Phishing is a technique where attackers trick individuals into revealing sensitive information such as passwords or banking details. Be cautious when opening emails, clicking unknown links, or providing personal information online. Verify email senders and never click on suspicious attachments.

3. Safe Social Media Practices

Limit the personal information you share on social media platforms. Set your profiles to private, use two-factor authentication wherever possible, and be cautious while accepting friend requests from unknown people.

4. Protecting Your Devices

Install reputable antivirus software, keep your device operating systems updated, and avoid downloading files or apps from unknown sources. Regularly back up your data to prevent loss in case of malware attacks.

5. Cyber Bullying Awareness

Cyber bullying can harm mental health and self-esteem. Report offensive content, block bullies, and support victims of online harassment. Schools, parents, and communities should educate youth on safe online behavior.
