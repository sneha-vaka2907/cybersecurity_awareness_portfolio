Cyber Security Awareness Tips

Here are some important tips to stay safe online:

  • Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
  • Don't share personal information with unknown sources
  • Always verify emails and links to avoid phishing scams
  • Update your software and apps regularly
  • Be cautious when using public Wi-Fi networks
  • Install antivirus and firewall protection
  • Backup your important data frequently
