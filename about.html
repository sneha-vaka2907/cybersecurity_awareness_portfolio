About Cyber Security

Cyber Security is all about protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks. In today's world, every individual should be aware of online safety practices to prevent cyber crimes.

Why Cyber Security Awareness is Important:

  • Protect your personal data from hackers
  • Prevent financial frauds and scams
  • Stay safe while browsing the internet
  • Understand how to secure your social media accounts
  • Be aware of phishing, malware, and other cyber threats
